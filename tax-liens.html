Common Questions About Tax Liens

How much back taxes can you pay off?

We've paid off tax debts from $5,000 to over $50,000. The amount doesn't matter - what matters is your home's value and equity. We handle it all at closing.

Do I need to catch up on taxes before selling?

No! We pay all back taxes at closing. You don't pay anything out of pocket. The taxes come out of the sale proceeds, and you keep the rest.

What if I'm already getting tax sale notices?

Call us immediately. We can often stop a tax sale even if it's scheduled. The sooner you call, the more options we have. Don't wait!

Will this hurt my credit?

Selling to us can actually help your credit. We pay off the tax lien, which is better than having a tax sale on your record. A sale is better than a seizure.

Can I still sell if I also have a mortgage?

Yes! We pay off both the mortgage and taxes. As long as there's enough equity to cover both, you'll still walk away with cash. We handle all the math.
