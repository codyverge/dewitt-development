Common Questions About Inherited Property

Do I need to complete probate before selling?

Not always. We work with many properties during probate. Every situation is different, but we can often structure a sale that works within Texas probate law. We're happy to work with your attorney.

What if the house is full of stuff?

That's completely normal and not a problem. Take what you want, leave the rest. We buy houses in any condition, including fully furnished with a lifetime of belongings.

How do you determine your offer price?

We look at the property's location, size, condition, and recent Tyler area sales. We factor in needed repairs and provide a fair cash offer. We're transparent about our numbers and happy to explain.

What if multiple family members inherited the property?

We work with this situation often. All heirs need to agree to the sale, and we make sure everyone is comfortable with the process. We can meet with everyone together or separately, whatever works best.

How quickly do I need to decide?

There's no rush. Our offers don't expire. We understand this is an emotional decision and you may need time to process, discuss with family, or handle other estate matters first.
